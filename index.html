About the Project

About SiteSpy

SiteSpy is a mobile-first construction project records and cost estimation app built with Expo React Native, Firebase, and Firestore. It helps construction teams capture measurements, estimate material costs, organise site records, and review project history on any device — even in low-connectivity field conditions.

My Contribution

My Contribution

My contribution focuses on the calculation engine that powers SiteSpy estimates. I implemented and reviewed calculations.js, which translates user-entered measurements into material estimates and cost projections. I also worked on estimateService.js to handle saving estimate sessions to Firestore and the EstimateSummaryScreen.js to make sure the saved results display clearly and correctly when users return to their projects.

Reflection

My Reflection

Getting the estimation formulas right was harder than I expected — small rounding errors in construction calculations can have large financial consequences. I learned to write unit-testable calculation helpers and to validate formula outputs against real-world material quantities. The save workflow taught me how Firestore transactions keep data consistent.
