About the Project

About SiteSpy

SiteSpy is a mobile-first construction project records and cost estimation app built with Expo React Native, Firebase, and Firestore. It helps construction teams capture measurements, estimate material costs, and review project history on any device — even in low-connectivity field conditions.

My Contribution

My Contribution

My contribution focuses on the calculation engine that powers SiteSpy estimates. I implemented and reviewed calculations.js, which translates user-entered measurements into material estimates and cost projections. I also worked on estimateService.js to handle saving estimate sessions to Firestore and EstimateSummaryScreen.js to make sure the saved results display clearly and correctly.
